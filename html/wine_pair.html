Wine Pairing

Wine pairing is a process of pairing food dishes with wine to enhance the dining experience. There are two types of pairings, the congruent pairing in which wines are paired with foods that are complementary in terms of their taste and contrasting pairing in which wines are paired with foods that enhances both the wine and the dish regardless of their contrast in taste.
